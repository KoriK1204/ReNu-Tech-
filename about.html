Our Mission

More Than Just
Refurbished Devices

At ReNu Tech, we're on a mission to close the loop on electronic waste. Every device we renew is one less gadget in a landfill and one more opportunity for someone to access premium Apple technology at an honest price.

We rigorously test, repair, and certify every product before it reaches your hands — because you deserve the same quality experience whether you're buying new or renewed.

Rigorous Testing

Every device passes a 72-point inspection covering hardware, software, battery health, and cosmetic condition.

Backed by Warranty

All products come with a 12-month warranty and 30-day return policy — no questions asked.

Sustainability First

Each renewed device saves approximately 70kg of CO₂ emissions compared to manufacturing a new one.

Fair Pricing

Premium quality at up to 40% off retail price. Great tech should be accessible to everyone.

Our Journey

How ReNu Tech Came to Be

From a small garage operation to a trusted name in refurbished Apple devices — here's our story.

2023 — The Beginning

Founded in a Garage

ReNu Tech started as a passion project, repairing old MacBooks for friends and family and selling them locally at fair prices.

2024 — Growing Up

First Official Store

We moved into our first workshop, hired our first two technicians, and launched our online storefront with a full iPhone and MacBook lineup.

2025 — Going Green

Sustainability Commitment

We formalized our environmental pledge — tracking every device diverted from landfill and partnering with eco-certified recyclers for non-repairable components.

2026 — Today

Trusted by Thousands

With over 5,000 devices renewed and a 98% customer satisfaction rate, ReNu Tech continues to grow as a trusted source for premium refurbished Apple products.
